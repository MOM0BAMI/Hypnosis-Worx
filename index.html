Meet Lara Bonetto

Hello! I’m Lara, an accredited Master Hypnotherapist (non-clinical), Master Transformation Coach, and Master Life Coach, based in Northcliff, Gauteng, South Africa. Since 2016, I’ve had the privilege of helping clients from all over the world – both in person and through online sessions – transform their lives.

My journey began as a Life Coach, and it reached new heights when I discovered the profound power of hypnosis. Becoming a Master Hypnotherapist was a pivotal moment and the results I’ve seen since have been nothing short of extraordinary, shattering boundaries and redefining possibilities. For clients who are open to working with hypnosis, I’ve found that it creates quicker, deeper, and more lasting results than coaching alone. It’s a powerful tool that truly accelerates change. Working with my clients is a dynamic and collaborative exchange of energy. I bring 100% focus, dedication, and expertise to guiding them, and I ask for their full commitment to achieving the changes they desire. Whether we’re addressing deep-seated challenges or fostering growth to move forward, success hinges on their willingness and dedication—a shared effort that drives transformative outcomes. I approach my work with precision and focus, but I also believe that transformation doesn’t have to be heavy or overly serious. Humor is a powerful ally in making the journey enjoyable and approachable. In our sessions, you’ll experience a balance: light-hearted, engaging conversations seamlessly paired with deep, meaningful work. The most common feedback I receive from my clients is how safe, supported, and "held" they feel during our time together—a true reflection of the nurturing environment I strive to create. What makes this work truly rewarding for me is witnessing the incredible transformations my clients achieve. Watching someone shift from deep anxiety to calm confidence, from fear and insecurity to embracing life with boldness, from confusion to clarity, or overcoming the hidden “monsters” of their subconscious is nothing short of inspiring. Seeing my clients reclaim their power, feel light and free, and step into the life they deserve fills me with gratitude and drives my passion every single day. I’m here to guide you through your own transformation, no matter where you’re starting from. Together, we’ll work towards your unique goals, be it clarity, calm or confidence, you’ve been seeking. Let’s make it happen!
